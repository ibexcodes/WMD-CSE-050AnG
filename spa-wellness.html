Our Wellness Facilities

Thermal Suite

Our state-of-the-art thermal area features a mosaic-tiled Hammam, aromatic steam room, Finnish sauna, and experiential showers. The perfect complement to any treatment or a rejuvenating experience on its own.

Guests can follow the traditional thermal circuit or create their own journey through the contrasting heat experiences, each designed to detoxify, improve circulation and promote deep relaxation.

Vitality Pool

The centerpiece of our spa is the stunning indoor-outdoor vitality pool with massage jets, swan neck fountains and a resistance channel. Maintained at body temperature year-round, this aquatic oasis promotes muscle relaxation and stress relief.

Surrounded by lush greenery and featuring panoramic views, our pool area includes heated loungers and a relaxation deck with premium daybeds for post-treatment unwinding.

Wellness Studio

Our fully equipped wellness studio offers daily yoga, Pilates and meditation classes, along with cutting-edge fitness equipment including Peloton bikes, Technogym cardio machines and free weights.

Personal training and wellness consultations are available with our expert instructors to help guests maintain their fitness routine or begin a new wellness journey during their stay.
